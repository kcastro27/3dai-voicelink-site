FAQ

Every Question Answered

Real questions from real service business owners.

Is this really an AI? Will my customers know?
+
Yes — 3DAi VoiceLink is an AI voice agent, and we're proud of it. What sets it apart is how knowledgeable, natural, and fast it is. It knows your business inside and out, answers questions confidently, books appointments, and qualifies leads — all in a conversation that feels genuinely helpful. Over 76% of callers are surprised by how natural it sounds. You can configure it to introduce itself however you'd like — most of our clients simply call it their "virtual assistant." The goal isn't to fool anyone. It's to make sure every caller gets an immediate, professional response — every single time.
What happens if the AI can't answer a question?
+
3DAi VoiceLink knows what it knows — and handles the rest gracefully. If a question falls outside its training, it apologizes naturally, lets the caller know you'll follow up personally, and keeps the conversation warm. No dead ends, no awkward silences — just a smooth experience every time.
Are there hidden fees? What does it really cost?
+
No hidden fees — ever. We believe in complete transparency. Choose the plan that fits your business, pay your monthly rate, and that's it. No per-call fees within your minute limit, no long-term contracts. If you're approaching your minute limit, we'll notify you before anything extra is charged. Simple, honest pricing — just like it should be.
How long does setup take?
+
Setup takes as long as it needs to — and not a minute less. Our team works directly with you to make sure your AI agent knows your business, speaks your language, and represents you exactly the way you want. We don't rush the process. We get it right. Most clients are live within 24 hours, but we stay with you until you're completely happy with every word it says.
What CRMs and tools does 3DAi VoiceLink connect to?
+
3DAi VoiceLink connects with Jobber, Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, Google Calendar, and most major CRMs. Don't see yours on the list? Our software engineering team is always working to expand what's possible. We're happy to explore custom integrations and will do our best to connect with the tools your business already depends on — just ask.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no questions asked. You can cancel any time directly from your dashboard. We're confident 3DAi VoiceLink will more than pay for itself — but we'd never trap you.
What if I need more minutes than my plan includes?
+
You'll receive an alert before you hit your limit. You can upgrade your plan at any time — instantly, no waiting period. Overage minutes are available at a straightforward per-minute rate if you need them before upgrading.
Does 3DAi VoiceLink work for my type of business?
+
If your business relies on phone calls to book jobs, answer questions, or capture leads — 3DAi VoiceLink was built for you. We serve plumbers, HVAC techs, roofers, landscapers, dentists, med spas, real estate agents, and many more. And because we're a full software engineering company, we don't stop at what's already built. If your business has specific needs that go beyond our standard offering, we want to hear about it. We're actively working with businesses to design and build custom solutions around their exact workflows. If you have a vision, we have the team to build it.
